At Auric we handcraft our jewelry in-house. Though we primarily produce gold jewelry we also make a handful of silver pieces within each collections. As a small business, we create our collections in small batches with sustainably and ethically sourced materials. Our pieces are made to be worn by anyone. With each product made to order, we aim to make elegant and graceful pieces that will match any occasion.
